What We Do

Learn About Our Process

Product Strategy

We believe in building softwarefor the real world rather than for an idea user. To achieve this we invite our clients and their customers to be part of our product development right from day 0. Our process is to go feature by feature, in quick sprints to ensure that we build the right tool fit for the market.

Interaction Design

Technology should be designed to reduce human effort; not complicate it. It should be easy to understand(without a manual). To ensure that the design/system feels natural to our users we focus and observe the users workflow and interate based on it.

Agile Development

Adapting and responding to change is the DE-facto requirement for developing a product fit for the market, hence we have made being agile a part of our culture at Red Panthers. We deploy the latest technology and practices to ensure rapid development with emphasis on stability and scalability.
